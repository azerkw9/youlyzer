Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2024

1. Information We Collect

Youlyzer collects minimal information necessary to provide our services. When you use our YouTube analysis tools, we process the YouTube URLs you submit but do not permanently store them. If you use our contact form, we collect your name, email address, subject, and message content.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely to provide and improve our services. YouTube URLs are processed in real-time to fetch video and channel data. Contact form submissions are stored securely and used only to respond to your inquiries.

3. Data Storage

Contact messages are stored in our database until manually deleted by administrators. We do not store YouTube video content permanently.

4. Third-Party Services

Our service interacts with YouTube (a Google service) to retrieve publicly available video and channel information using the official YouTube Data API. We do not share your personal information with third parties except as required by law.

5. Cookies

Youlyzer uses essential cookies required for the functioning of our website (such as CSRF protection tokens and session management). We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, please contact us through our contact page.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us.
